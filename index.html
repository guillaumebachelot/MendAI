Our Mission

Every patient deserves
high-precision testing

Studying our genes alone cannot explain all the diseases that develop over a lifetime. While genomics maps potential risks, proteins and small molecules reflect the patient's real-time biological state.

Diagnostic tests based on measuring these molecules have already been shown to triple survival rates for certain types of cancer. Yet spectrometry — the core technology behind these tests — remains challenging to master, limiting widespread clinical adoption.

"We believe every patient deserves high-precision testing to better understand their biological state and guide more informed medical decisions."

PRISM

Advanced Signal Processing

Analyzes raw signals to extract clinically relevant information.

  • Analyzes entire batches — not samples individually — to correct statistical biases and instrument drift
  • Continuously learns locally from user feedback
  • Automates up to 90% of signal processing
+$600k / year per clinical platform*
HELIX

Real-Time Control Engine

Autonomous instrument control that continuously optimizes analytical methods.

  • Leverages Bayesian optimization to dynamically adjust acquisition parameters in real time
  • Autonomously refines experimental methods without human intervention
  • Transforms method development into an autonomous process
+$300k / year in method development*
GENESIS

AI-Driven Method Design

Automatically generates sample preparation protocols and acquisition methods.

  • LLM-based agent connected to the scientific literature and manufacturers' application notes
  • Reduces method development from days to minutes
  • No prior expert configuration required
Minutes vs. days of development
